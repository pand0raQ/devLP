# SEO Strategy for `pand0raq.dev`

## Goal

Increase qualified organic traffic to the two priority apps:

- `Couple Status`
- `Timecostify: Cost in Time`

## What Was Implemented

### 1. Better topic architecture

- Repositioned the homepage around searchable app categories instead of a generic portfolio.
- Strengthened the branded product pages:
  - `/couplestatus/`
  - `/timecostify/`
- Added two non-branded search-entry pages:
  - `/long-distance-couple-app/`
  - `/partner-availability-app/`
  - `/couples-mood-tracker-widget/`
  - `/price-in-work-hours-calculator/`

### 2. Stronger intent matching

The site now covers problem-led searches, not just brand-led searches.

Primary topic targets:

- `couples mood tracker`
- `partner status app`
- `partner availability app`
- `relationship widget iphone`
- `long distance couple app`
- `price in work hours calculator`
- `cost in time calculator`
- `salary to hours calculator`
- `mindful spending app`

### 3. Stronger technical SEO

- Added canonical tags to key pages.
- Added richer JSON-LD markup.
- Added internal links between homepage, product pages, and intent pages.
- Added new pages to `vite.config.js` and `public/sitemap.xml`.
- Updated `llms.txt` and `llms-full.txt`.
- Added a lightweight interactive calculator for the Timecostify topic cluster.

## Success Metrics

- Growth in impressions and clicks for non-branded queries.
- Rankings for the new intent pages.
- App Store click-through rate from organic landing pages.
- Indexed page count for the new URLs.

## Constraints

- No live keyword volume or competitor data was gathered in this implementation pass.
- This work improves SEO readiness and intent coverage, but rankings will still depend on indexing, backlinks, and content expansion.
