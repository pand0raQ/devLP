About this app

How many hours do you need to work for that coffee? That new phone? Timecostify shows you the real cost of everything you buy.

• See Prices as Time

Enter any price and instantly see how many work hours it costs. Just add your hourly, monthly, or yearly wage—we'll handle the math.

• Home Screen Widgets

Check costs without opening the app. Tap "Buy" or "Don't Buy" right from your home screen to track your decisions.

• Track Your Savings

Watch how small decisions add up. See your progress with simple charts that keep you motivated.

• Any Currency

Shopping abroad or online? We convert currencies automatically so you always know what you're really spending.

• Custom Schedule

Set your actual working hours—whether you work 30 hours a week or 60, your calculations will be accurate.

• Household Mode

Calculate for yourself or your whole household. Switch modes anytime.

Why It Works:

A $60 dinner = 3 hours of work. Suddenly, it feels different, doesn't it? We're not here to tell you what to buy. We just show you what it costs in time, so you can decide what's worth it.
