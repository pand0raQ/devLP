About this app

How many hours do you need to work for that coffee? That new phone? Timecostify is a personal finance utility that converts abstract money prices into concrete work time, changing your spending habits and mindset.

• See Prices as Time

Enter any price and instantly see how many work hours it costs. Just add your hourly, monthly, or yearly wage—we'll handle the math automatically.

• Home Screen Widgets

Check costs without opening the app. Tap "Buy" or "Don't Buy" right from your Home Screen to track your daily purchasing decisions.

• Track Your Savings

Watch how small decisions add up. Review your financial progress over time with clean charts that keep you motivated.

• Multi-Currency Support

Shopping online or traveling abroad? We convert currencies automatically so you always know what you're really spending.

• Custom Work Schedules & Household Mode

Set your actual working hours—whether you work 30 or 60 hours a week, calculations remain accurate. Switch to Household Mode to calculate totals for your entire home.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Timecostify calculate time cost?

Timecostify takes the price of any product and divides it by your hourly rate (calculated from your salary and work hours schedule). For example, if you make $30/hour, a $120 dinner represents 4 hours of work.

Can I track my spending decisions on the Home Screen?

Yes! Timecostify features interactive Home Screen widgets that let you tap "Buy" or "Don't Buy" directly, updating your saving graphs immediately.

Does the app support currency conversion?

Yes. It includes built-in currency support to fetch current exchange rates, enabling automatic conversions for online purchases or travel.
