Frequently Asked Questions

What is Couple Status?

Couple Status is an iOS app designed for couples to sync their real-time mood and activity directly onto their iPhone Lock Screens using modern widgets, helping them stay connected ambiently.

How does the mood and status sync work?

Once you connect with your partner via a unique pairing link, you can choose from default activities like "Doing Chores", "Hobby", or "Focused Work", or update your custom mood. The app automatically pushes updates to your partner's widgets in real-time.

Do we need to buy two separate subscriptions?

No! Couple Status uses a "pay once, use together" pricing model. A single subscription unlocks Premium access for both partners.

What are the compatibility requirements?

Couple Status is fully optimized for iOS devices running iOS 26.0 or later, and Apple Watches running watchOS 26.0 or later.
