Frequently asked questions

These answers are written for people comparing couple communication apps, relationship widgets, and shared status tools.

What is Couple Status?

Couple Status is an iPhone app that lets partners share mood, activity, and availability using real-time updates and widgets.

How does Couple Status differ from normal messaging apps?

It focuses on quick, ambient visibility rather than full conversations. The goal is to reduce repeated check-in texts while keeping emotional closeness.

Is Couple Status good for long-distance relationships?

Yes. It works well for couples who want more day-to-day presence without pressure to reply constantly.

Is Couple Status a partner availability app?

Yes. The app is designed to show availability, focus state, mood, and activity so couples can communicate with better timing and fewer interruptions.

Can both partners use one subscription?

Yes. The app uses a pay once, use together model so one purchase covers both partners.
