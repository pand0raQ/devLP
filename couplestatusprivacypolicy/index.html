Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Abdukhalik Magomedov ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: To use certain features of the App, you may provide a name or nickname and link your account with your partner.
  • Status Updates: The core functionality of the App involves sharing your current status (e.g., "Work", "Free", "Busy") with your linked partner. This data is collected and synchronized to enable the service.

B. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the App, such as button clicks, screen views, and other interactions (e.g., via Mixpanel or Google Analytics).
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to ensure proper app functionality and troubleshooting.
  • Widget Data: If you use our widgets, status information is processed to display updates on your home or lock screen.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Facilitate the real-time synchronization of status updates between you and your partner.
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance user experience.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages (if applicable).

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Partner: Your status updates and relevant profile info are shared with the user you have explicitly linked with.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party vendors (e.g., Firebase for backend services, Mixpanel for analytics) to perform services on our behalf. These vendors access your data only to perform these tasks and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. Status history may be stored temporarily to provide the most recent updates to your partner.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no transmission over the Internet or electronic storage method is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page or within the App.
